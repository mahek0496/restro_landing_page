Our Story

Nestled in the heart of the city, The Velvet Cafe is a culinary sanctuary where passion meets perfection. Founded in 2015 by master chef Rajiv Malhotra, our restaurant has become synonymous with exquisite flavors and unforgettable dining experiences.

Our philosophy is simple: to create dishes that tell a story. Each recipe is crafted with generations of culinary wisdom, using only the finest locally-sourced ingredients. From the aromatic spices in our biryanis to the fresh herbs in our mocktails, every element is carefully selected to delight your senses.
